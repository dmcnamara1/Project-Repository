    What is Synpact?

    Synpact is an online media company and socially conscious platform, dedicated to making a positive impact in the world. The brand acts as a middleman service, connecting people to different charities and nonprofits that are addressing modern world issues. We want to bring people together in an attempt to help solve an endless variety of causes, while also providing incentives and benefits to the people who participate. Our goal is to revolutionize the charity industry and take an alternate approach on philanthropy through community, entertainment, rewards, and transparency.
